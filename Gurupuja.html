Welcome to Rangoli Arts

Rangoli is a traditional Indian art form that involves drawing colorful patterns on the floor or entrances of homes The rangoli represents the happiness, positivity and liveliness of a household, and is intended to welcome Lakshmi, the goddess of wealth and good luck. It is believed that a Hindu household without a clean entrance and rangoli is an abode of daridra (bad luck). The purpose of rangoli is beyond decoration.
